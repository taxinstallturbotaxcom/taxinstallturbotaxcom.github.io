Which TurboTax Version is Right for You?

Choosing the right TurboTax version depends on your tax needs:

  • Basic: Simple returns with no dependents.
  • Deluxe: Ideal for itemized deductions.
  • Premier: For investments and rental properties.
  • Home & Business: Self-employed or small business owners.
  • Business: Partnerships, S corporations, and C corporations.

How to Decide?

  1. Use Free Edition if your return is straightforward.
  2. Opt for Deluxe if you want to maximize deductions.
  3. Choose Premier if you deal with investments or rental properties.
  4. Select Self-Employed if you own a business or freelance.
  5. Pick Business for corporate or partnership tax needs.

Installing TurboTax on Windows and Mac

For Windows:
  1. Download TurboTax: Visit InstallTurboTax.com and log in with your account. Enter your license code and download the software.
  2. Locate the Downloaded File: Find the downloaded file, typically in your "Downloads" folder.
  3. Run the Installer: Double-click the downloaded file to start the installation. Follow the on-screen instructions.
  4. Enter License Code: When prompted, enter your license code.
  5. Finish Installation: Complete the installation process and start TurboTax.

For Mac:
  1. Download TurboTax: Visit InstallTurboTax.com and log in with your account. Enter your license code and download the software.
  2. Locate the Downloaded File: Find the downloaded file, usually in your "Downloads" folder.
  3. Open the Installer: Double-click the .dmg file to open the installer.
  4. Drag to Applications: Drag the TurboTax icon into the Applications folder.
  5. Launch TurboTax: Open TurboTax from the Applications folder. Enter your license code when prompted.
  6. Complete Installation: Follow the on-screen instructions to finish the installation.
